Challenges

Working on a portfolio project can be both exciting and challenging. Below are some of the common challenges faced during the process:

Key Challenges

  • Time Management: Balancing project work with other responsibilities.
  • Design Consistency: Ensuring a cohesive and professional design throughout the portfolio.
  • Technical Issues: Debugging and resolving unexpected coding problems.
  • Content Creation: Writing clear and engaging descriptions of our work.
  • Lack of Inspiration: Finding unique and creative ways to present your skills.
  • Collaboration conflicts: We created branches for each member to push his/her code, later merge the codes in the master branch.
